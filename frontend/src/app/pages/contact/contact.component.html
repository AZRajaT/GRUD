Delivery Terms & Conditions

At AROW Mart, we strive to provide fast, fresh, and reliable grocery delivery to your doorstep.

Delivery Coverage

We currently deliver to specific areas within Chennai. You can check service availability for your location by entering your PIN code on our website or app.

Delivery Timings

  • Same-Day Delivery: For orders placed before 12:00 PM
  • Next-Day Delivery: For orders placed after 12:00 PM
  • Deliveries are made between 9:00 AM and 9:00 PM

Delivery Charges

  • Free Delivery for orders above ₹1999
  • Orders below ₹1999 may attract a delivery fee of ₹30 to ₹50, depending on your location

Missed Deliveries

If you are not available at the time of delivery:

  • Our delivery team will try to contact you
  • If unreachable, we may reschedule once or cancel the order as per our policy

Order Verification

Please inspect the delivered items at the time of receipt. Report any missing or damaged items to our support team within 2 hours of delivery.
